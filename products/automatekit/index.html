FAQs

1. What are the sensors available in Add-on for Automate Starter Kit?

+

Add-on for Automate Starter Kit is for the users who have already purchased the Starter Kit and want to upgrade it to Advanced Kit. (1) Light sensor, (1) Temperature sensor, (1) Moisture sensor, (2) IR sensor, (1) PIR sensor, (1) Sound sensor are the sensors available in the Add-on for Automate Starter Kit.

2. Is Automate Kit suitable for the kids who have not done coding before?

+

Yes. Automate Kit is the perfect kit for 10+ year old kids who have never done any coding before. Since Automate Kit promote unique and game changing concept of 'Programming without Coding', Kids can start exploring the projects and program their logic, right from the start without any inhibitions.

3. What are the projects available in Automate Starter Kit?

+

Basic remote-control car, Dump Truck, Obstacle avoiding robot, Robot Soccer game, Obstacle-avoiding robot with fail-safe mechanism, Light On/Off using relay, Light On/Off using relay(Alarm)
