            # Light bulb on/off with remote/timer

            # Introduction

            In this project, with KiMu's Automate Kit, we are going to build a system using relay switch to turn on/off the light bulb at the specific time automatically or to turn on/off light bulb with remote control.

            2. Logical Thinking
