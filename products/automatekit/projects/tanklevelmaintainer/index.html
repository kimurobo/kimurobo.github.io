              # Assemble the System

              Step 1: Place the submersible pump in the container that has water in it.
              Step 2: Connect one end of the transparent hose to the submersible pump and the other end to the empty container.
              Step 3: Connect the moisture sensor to the U1 connector of the sensor board.
              Step 4: Place the moisture sensor along with the sensor board in the empty container.
