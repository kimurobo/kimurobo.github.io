            # Tank Level Maintainer

            # Introduction

            With KiMu's Automate Advanced Kit, we are going to build a system to automatically maintain the water level in the tank. When the water level in the tank falls below a certain value, the pump will start automatically, transferring water to the tank from another reserve tank. A moisture sensor is utilized in this project to determine the water level.

            # Key skills/Learnings

            1. Problem Solving
            2. Logical Thinking
            3. Working of moisture sensor & submersible pump

            Step 1: Place the submersible pump in the container that has water in it.

            Step 3: Connect the moisture sensor to the U1 connector of the sensor board.
